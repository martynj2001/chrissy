Chrissy Jones - Holistic Therapist MFHT

Who am I...

I was introduced to Complementary Therapies as a child when I was treated for severe headaches is suffered following a road traffic accident by an osteopath. Over the following years i turned to other therapies for various reasons and i have received chiropractic care, massage therapy and during a particularly turbulent time in my life i came across and fell in love with reflexology. Following my marriage and the subsequent relocation of our home i decided it was time to leave my previous career in HR and Recruitment and embark on a new path. I initially qualified in Swedish Massage in 2010. This was followed by qualifications in Indian Head Massage, Pregnancy Massage and Reflexology. I offer these treatments as a mobile therapist or from my home. On a personal note I have two children and a dog and i love spending time with the family and exploring new areas of the country in our campervan.

Reflexology

A gentle yet powerful holistic treatment.

Reflexology is a gentle yet powerful holistic treatment involving the stimulation of specific points on the feet (or hands) that correspond to certain parts of the body. By stimulating these points you promote the bodies natural healing process which in turn enables nature balance and physical wellbeing. Benefits include:

  • Improved sleep
  • Regulate hormone imbalance
  • Relieve constipation
  • Reduce headaches
  • Can help with anxiety and depression
  • Create a general feeling of wellbeing
Treatments are clothed in a reclined chair, they last approcimately 1 hour.
