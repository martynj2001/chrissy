Who am I...

I was introduced to complementary therapies as a child when I saw an Osteopath for treatment for severe headaches following a road traffic accident. Over the years I have often used complimentary therapies for both relaxation and for my general health and well being. In 2009 I decided to move away from a previous career in HR and recruitment and follow my dream of becoming a complementary therapist. I initially qualified in Swedish Massage in 2010. This was followed by qualifications in Indian Head Massage and Reflexology. I am able to offer these treatments from a dedicated room in my home or alternatively as a mobile therapist. I am married and have two children and a dog and I love spending time with the family and exploring new areas of the country in our campervan.
